The workflow

8 steps. Fully automated.

Submit a form. Walk away. Come back to a sheet full of leads.

01
You fill in the filter form
Choose your target: location, industry, job title, company size, revenue range. Hit submit.
02
Make.com kicks off the chain
Your filters are received instantly and the automation starts running — no manual trigger needed.
03
Apify scrapes 2,500+ leads
The Leads Finder actor searches the internet and pulls a verified list of matching companies and decision-makers.
04
System reads each company website
For every lead, an HTTP request visits their site and pulls the content. Like a researcher doing pre-call homework.
05
GPT summarizes the company
ChatGPT processes the website content and writes a clean, useful summary of what the company does and where they are.
06
GPT writes a personalized opener
Using the summary, GPT writes a 2-sentence cold email opening specific to that company. Swap the niche prompt in 30 seconds.
07
Apify verifies every email New
The Email Verifier actor checks every address. Invalid emails are flagged. You only send to confirmed, deliverable addresses. Costs $1 per 1,000 — covered by Apify's free $5/month.
08
Everything saves to Google Sheets
Name, email, title, LinkedIn, company summary, personalized icebreaker, and verification status — all in one clean row per lead.
